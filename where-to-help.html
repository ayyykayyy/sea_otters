Donate to the Monterey Bay Aquarium

The Monteray Bay Aquarium has been a epicenter of sea otter recovery. They dedicate themselves to help care for sick sea otters and their sea otters' help orphan and raise stray otter pups so they can be released back into the wild. More information about their goals and donations can be made on their website.

Adopt a rescue animal from
Vancouver Aquarium

The Vancouver Aquarium accepts donations as well as "adopting" a rescue animal, where for a fee they will send you a patient fact card, an adoption certificate, a tax receipt for your purchase, and a option for an e-card to someone. They have many animals in this program, including Sea Otters!
