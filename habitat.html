Sea Otters!

About Habitat How to Help

Southern sea otters can be found along California's central coast, from San Mateo County in the north to near Santa Barbara in the south. Sea Otter are a keystone species in the kelp forests. they eat sea urchins and other animals that graze on giant kelp. This helps keeps the Sea Urchin population under control. Sea otters also keep eelgrass healthy by eating crabs, which in turn allows the sea slug population to thrive. These sea slugs then eat algae that would otherwise coat and smother the eelgrass that fish need for food and shelter.

Sea otters like to hold hands with other sea otters while resting to create these "rafts" of sea otters. These rafts are gender-based, meaning boy otters will hold hands with other boy otter and girl otters will hold hands with other girl otters.
